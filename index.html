About me

I am a technologist and consultant based in Boston, MA.

I graduated from Cornell University in Operations Research & Information Engineering in 2011. I relocated to Boston to help solve difficult problems in healthcare. Currently, I am afull-stack Ruby on Rails hacker developing an accelerator for healthcare provider change management.

Previously, I worked on T3, a real-time physiological data visualization and analysis webapp, striving to improve rapid clinical decision-making at Boston Children's Hospital ICUs.
